About PathShine

PathShine is a learning platform that breaks down complex skills into manageable, step-by-step roadmaps.

Our Mission

We believe learning should be structured, trackable, and accessible to everyone. Our roadmaps are designed to take you from complete beginner to proficient practitioner, with clear milestones along the way.

Features

  • Step-by-step learning roadmaps
  • Progress tracking and motivation
  • Curated resources from trusted sources
  • Mobile-friendly responsive design
  • Local progress storage (no account needed)
  • Dark mode support
  • Multiple programming languages
